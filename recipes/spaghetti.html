Spaghetti & Meatsauce

  • 1lb. Ground Beef
  • 2 cups homemade roasted tomato sauce
  • 6oz tomato paste
  • 14.5oz diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup Chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon basil
  • 1 tablespoon parsely
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • Splash of wine
  • Toppings: Fresh basil pepperoni, black olives, and parmesan cheese
  1. Add ground beef to a large deep skillet or pot.
  2. Cook until browned
  3. Drain off the fat from the beef
  4. Stir in roasted tomato sauce, tomato paste and diced tomatoes
  5. Stir in broth and water
  6. Add bay leaf and red pepper flakes
  7. Cover and simmer gently for 1 hour
  8. Add brown sugar
  9. Add fresh herbs and a splash of wine
  10. Taste the sauce and add salt, pepper or other seasonings if desired
  11. Serve sauce over spaghetti noodles and garnish with toppings.
